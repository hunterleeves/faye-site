Faye

privacy policy

effective July 13, 2026

Faye works with some of the most personal things there are — photos of your body, your measurements, sometimes the fact that you’re pregnant or postpartum. So this policy is written to be actually read: plain English, no burying the important parts.

the short version

  • Your photos, chats, and style profile are stored only on your phone, encrypted. We have no copy and no way to look at them.
  • When you send Faye a message, that message — plus your profile details — travels securely to Anthropic, the company whose AI writes Faye’s replies. It is not used to train AI and we don’t store it afterward.
  • On our servers we keep exactly two things: your sign-in account and a daily count of how many messages you send. That’s all.
  • No ads, no analytics trackers, no selling or sharing data with anyone. Ever.

what stays on your phone

Everything Faye knows about you lives on your device and nowhere else:

  • your outfit photos and saved outfit wins
  • your full chat history with Faye
  • your style profile — height, weight, Kibbe body type, color seasons, style icons, style words, and whether you’re currently dressing a pregnant or postpartum body

These are written to your phone’s storage with iOS file protection, meaning they’re encrypted and unreadable while your phone is locked. They are never uploaded to any server, gallery, or account of ours. There is no dashboard or database where anyone — including the person who built Faye — can browse your photos or conversations. If you delete the app, all of it is gone for good.

what happens when you message Faye

Faye’s replies are written by Claude, an AI made by Anthropic. When you send a message or photo, it travels over an encrypted connection through our server to Anthropic, along with your style profile — that’s how the advice gets personalized to your body type, colors, and life stage rather than being generic.

Our server acts only as a relay: it checks that you’re signed in, counts the message toward your daily limit, and passes it along. It does not save the content of your messages or photos.

Anthropic processes your message to generate the reply. Under Anthropic’s API terms, your messages and photos are not used to train their AI models. You can read Anthropic’s own privacy policy at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

what we store on our servers

  • Your account. Faye uses Sign in with Apple. We receive a unique account ID and the email address you choose to share (which can be Apple’s private relay address if you choose “Hide My Email”). We never see a password — there isn’t one.
  • A daily message count. A number per account per day, used to enforce a fair-use limit and keep the beta free. Never the content of the messages — just how many.

That is the complete list. There are no analytics SDKs, ad trackers, or third-party data brokers in the app.

sensitive information, plainly

Because Faye is a styling app, you may share body photos, your weight, and pregnancy or postpartum status. This information is used for exactly one thing — generating your styling advice — and it follows the rules above: stored only on your phone, sent transiently to Anthropic when you message, never stored on our servers, never used for anything else, never shared or sold.

deleting your data

  • Photos, chats, and profile: delete the app from your phone and they’re permanently gone. We have no copy to delete.
  • Your account: email hunterleevescodes@gmail.com and we’ll delete your account and its message counts, typically within a few days. In-app account deletion is coming before full release.

who we work with

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, and TestFlight during the beta.
  • Supabase — hosts our sign-in system and the message counter.
  • Anthropic — the AI that writes Faye’s replies.

These are service providers processing data on Faye’s behalf; none of them are permitted to use your data for their own purposes beyond providing the service. We do not sell or share personal information with anyone else, full stop.

this website

This site is a static page with no cookies, no analytics, and no accounts. Its fonts load from Google Fonts, which means your browser makes a standard request to Google’s servers when the page loads.

children

Faye is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, email us and we’ll delete it.

changes & contact

If this policy changes in a way that matters, we’ll update this page and its effective date, and note the change in the app’s release notes. Questions about any of it: hunterleevescodes@gmail.com.
